Privacy Policy

App: Slenora

Slenora is a sleep analytics mobile application (the "App"). The App is developed and created by Ridith Shetty (the "Developer"), a minor, and is legally published, operated, and represented by his parent and legal guardian, Santhosh Shetty (the "Publisher"), based in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when users access or use the App.

By creating an account, signing in, or using the App, you consent to the collection and processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. For the purposes of this policy, "we," "us," or "our" refers collectively to the Publisher and the Developer.

Information Collected

Depending on how the app is used, Slenora may collect or process the following information:

  • Google Sign-In Information: Limited strictly to the user's name and email address for account creation and authentication.
  • Sleep-Related Data: Sleep start time, sleep end time, sleep duration, sleep disturbances, sleep scores, sleep history, and related statistical analysis results.
  • User-Submitted Content: Custom sleep notes and logs explicitly entered by the user.
  • Technical Account Data: Authentication and account tokens necessary to securely operate the account and synchronize saved history across devices using Supabase backend services.

Microphone and Audio Processing

Important Privacy Note on Audio Data: Slenora utilizes device microphone access during active sleep tracking sessions solely to detect snoring and analyze sleep-related audio patterns. Raw microphone audio is processed strictly on-device in real-time. We do not transmit, stream, or store raw audio recordings on our servers or via Supabase.

Only the calculated, anonymous numeric results of the audio analysis (such as snoring intervals, duration, and summaries) are synchronized to your account database.

Where Data Is Stored

While raw audio data stays local to your device, your account profile details, calculated sleep analysis scores, statistics, custom notes, and sleep logs are securely processed and stored using Supabase infrastructure. This enables you to safely retrieve and review your historical sleep data across sessions or if you switch devices.

What Slenora Does Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store financial payment card details through the app.
  • We do not collect, intercept, or upload personal voice recordings or background conversations.

How Information Is Used

We use the data we collect for the following specific purposes:

  • To provide and maintain core app features, including sleep tracking, snoring metrics, and score generation.
  • To securely authenticate your profile using Google Sign-In.
  • To compile, save, and visualize your past sleep history and dashboard insights.
  • To respond to user support emails, data inquiries, and account deletion requests.
  • To diagnose technical bugs, optimize backend stability, and improve overall app functionality.

Legal Basis and Consent

Slenora relies on explicit user consent and lawful bases permitted under applicable legal frameworks (including relevant Indian data protection provisions) to process your data. Permissions like microphone access are strictly voluntary and requested dynamically inside the app with clear context before use.

Third-Party Services

We utilize Supabase as our core backend database and data storage service provider. Third-party providers process information strictly as necessary to support and maintain the computing infrastructure required by Slenora.

Data Sharing

Slenora does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or marketers. Information may be disclosed only under the following limited conditions:

  • With cloud infrastructure service providers (like Supabase) bound by confidentiality terms to assist app operation.
  • When required by law, valid regulation, a court order, or an official, lawful government request.

Data Retention

Account profiles and sleep metrics are retained for as long as your user account remains active to provide continuous access to your historical dashboard. If you request account closure, personal data will be wiped from active databases within a reasonable administrative timeframe, unless storage is required by legal or compliance obligations.

Account and Data Deletion

You retain complete control over your data. Users may request the permanent deletion of their account profile and all historical sleep analytics data at any time by contacting our team directly at slenora.app@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Slenora is designed for a general audience and does not explicitly market to or direct features toward children under the age of 13. Because the developer is a minor, the legal responsibilities and administration of this data architecture are completely managed under the oversight of the adult Publisher.

Data Security

We implement standard, industry-accepted security protocols through Supabase to safeguard your information. However, please be aware that no modern electronic storage system or network transit protocol can be certified as 100% infallible, and absolute security cannot be fully guaranteed.

International Users

Slenora is built and operated out of India. If you use the app from regions outside of India, your information will be processed and securely stored in jurisdictions where our third-party infrastructure providers operate servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
